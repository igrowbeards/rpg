Skill and Ability Checks
========================

Skill and ability checks are made versus a target number determined by how difficult the task is.

<dl>
	<dt>trivial</dt>
	<dd>2</dd>
	<dt>easy</dt>
	<dd>4</dd>
	<dt>moderate</dt>
	<dd>5</dd>
	<dt>challenging</dt>
	<dd>8</dd>
	<dt>difficult</dt>
	<dd>10</dd>
	<dt>extremely difficult</dt>
	<dd>12</dd>
	<dt>incredibly difficult</dt>
	<dd>15</dd>
</dl>
